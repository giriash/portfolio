Ithaca Falls on Fall Creek

Experience one of Ithaca's secret waterfalls this summer, a great place to stop and enjoy a picnic lunch or just relax with a good book! With a 150ft cascading fall, watch Fall Creek's final plunge to Cayuga Lake.

Location: Lake St., Ithaca, NY 14850

Phone: 607-272-1313
Open time: The trail is open year-round.
Difficulty: Easy

Taughannock Falls State Park

Taughannock falls carves a 400ft deep gorge through layers of sandstone, shale and limestone that were once the bed of an ancient sea. With a 215 foot plunge, this waterfall stands three stories taller than Niagara Falls.

Location: 1740 Taughannock Blvd., (Rt 89), Trumansburg, NY 14886

Phone: 607-387-6739
Open time: Gorge trail and overlook open year-round. Others open April-Oct.
Difficulty: Moderate

Robert H. Treman State Park

Buttermilk Creek descends more than 500 feet in a series of cascades and rapids. A natural pool at the base of Buttermilk Falls provides swimming with the feel of an old swimming hole.

Location: Rte 327, RD 10 Ithaca, NY 14850

Phone: 607-273-5761
Difficulty: Easy to Difficult

Businessman's Lunch Falls on Six Mile Creek

Businessman's Lunch Fallls is a set of four cascading falls - 30ft, 5ft, 15ft, 15f - and is popular lunch spot for downtown office workers.

Location: Giles St., Ithaca, NY 14850

Open time: Open year round, but often impassible in winter due to ice.
Difficulty: Easy

Buttermilk Falls State Park

The park features six trails with spectacular rocky gorges and wooded sections. A three mile section of the Finger Lakes Trail goes through the park.

Location: 112 E. Buttermilk Falls Rd., off Rt 13 S, Ithaca, NY 14850

Phone: 607-273-3440
Open time: Falls view open year round. Trails open April-Oct.
Difficulty: Moderate

Ludlowville Falls on Salmon Creek

Salmon Creek pours over a hard limestone cap to form beautiful Ludlowville Falls. The soft shale underneath has eroded away, creating an overhang cave and deep plunge pool.

Location: Salmon Creek Rd., Lansing, NY 14882

Open time: Falls overlook open year round. Trails closed in winter.
Difficulty: Easy
