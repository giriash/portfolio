Fresh Experience in Ithaca, NY

In our corner of the Finger Lakes Region of New York, we've become a destination known for cultural events, activities and outstanding outdoor recreation. Our population is infused with the constant energy of youth from the bright students and faculty of Ithaca College and Cornell University, making it a unique and dynamic vacation destination that is charming, intelligent and varied. Ithaca is more of an oasis than a destination, more discovery than itinerary. Enjoy your trip!

  • Things to Do

What you'll discover in Ithaca could be something fleeting, like a rainbow in the mist of one of Ithaca's many waterfalls. Or maybe something powerful, like insight from an acclaimed speaker on campus at Cornell University. It could taste sweet, like the fresh honey at the Ithaca Farmers Market. Ithaca's featured attractions will keep you busy in our Finger Lakes town. Bring all your senses. And your camera.

  • Places to Stay

There's a bumper sticker here that says Ithaca, NY is 10 square miles surrounded by reality. It's mostly true, and it nicely describes the atmosphere you'll find once you settle into one of our comfortable Ithaca hotels. Whether you fancy a stay at a homey B&B, sophisticated small inn, or full-service luxury resort, you'll soon discover that our lodging accommodations specialize in relaxation.

  • Plan Your Trip

Ithaca has so many attractions, activities, restaurants that you can experience. Plan your own Ithaca trip now by using the 'Build Your Trip' tool to search information and schedule your trip. Or click to see the classic routes of Ithaca and routes particularly made for your type as well as group tour information. Download brochures and get transportation information before you come.
