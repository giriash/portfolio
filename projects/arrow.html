Arrow - MOBILE APP

MY ROLE: UI/UX Design

TEAM MEMBERS: Alexander Panos, software engineering team memners

PROJRCT DESCRIPTION:
Arrow is a mobile app for Android and iOS that allows you to keep track of where your friends are on beautiful, custom venue maps through GPS. Built for music festivals, users create a group “Bubble”, and add their friends. Arrow helps users easily navigate through the chaos and find their friends through the use of a compass “Arrow”. Users can also message their Bubble, individual friends, and take photos/videos which appear on the map and on a media stream. Custom venue maps for music festivals also show stage/vendor/medical tent locations, notify users when an artist is about to perform (along with other important info), and allow the user to ping exact GPS location to venue staff in event of an emergency.
